What is Games Unite?

Games Unite is a Roblox shooter that's heavily inspired by the Source Engine.

State of the Game

Games Unite is still early in development, and currently just a testing place. The testing place will be updated into the full game.

Features

What sets Games Unite apart from all the other roblox shooters? Why not just go play what you already know?

Source Movement

Movement inspired by old games and engines such as Quake and the Source Engine. Bhopping, strafing, and surfing included.

Custom Engine

Although not technically a new engine since it's still Roblox, Games Unite has essentially a completely custom engine. From movement to physics.

Customizability

Change your viewmodel offset, turn on and off your arms, change as many crosshair settings as you'd like. Extreme customizability with the commands GUI.

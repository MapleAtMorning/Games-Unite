What is Games Unite?

Games Unite is a Roblox shooter that's made to mimic the source engine

Features

What sets Games Unite apart from all the other roblox shooters? Why not just go play what you already know?

Source Movement

Movement made specifically to replicate the source engine. Bhopping, strafing, and surfing included.

Custom Engine

Humanoids? Nah. Games Unite has essentially a completely custom engine, from movement to physics.

Customizability

Change your viewmodel offset, turn on and off your arms, change as many crosshair settings as you'd like. Extreme customizability with the commands GUI.
